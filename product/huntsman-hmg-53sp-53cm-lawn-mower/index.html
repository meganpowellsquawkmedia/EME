Revolutionise your lawn care routine with the Huntsman HMG-53SP lawn mower, a powerful and versatile machine designed to bring efficiency and precision to your gardening endeavors. With its impressive 53 cm (21”) cutting width, this mower covers more ground with each pass, ensuring that your lawn gets the attention it deserves in no time.

Experience the convenience of self-propelled propulsion, making mowing a breeze and saving you valuable time and energy. The 8-stage height adjustment provides complete control over your grass length, ranging from 20 mm for a perfectly manicured look to 75 mm for a lush, well-maintained lawn.

Equipped with a robust 196cc Loncin engine and a reliable pull-start mechanism, the HMG-53SP is built for power and endurance. No more struggles to get your mower started; this machine kicks into action smoothly, ready to tackle any lawn care challenge.

Choose between grass collection for a tidy finish or side discharge for a natural, nutrient-rich mulching effect. The 70-liter collection bag ensures fewer interruptions, allowing you to focus on the task at hand. The sturdy steel deck adds durability, promising a long-lasting partnership in maintaining your outdoor oasis.
