Description The Lenco PA-220 is a Bluetooth party speaker that gives you huge sound, convenient wireless streaming, and attention-grabbing lighting effects. It includes a large 10-inch subwoofer for huge, rumbling bass, and a tweeter column to make sure your high-end remains detailed and present, giving it a punchy sound. Then, set the tone for your party with built-in lighting effects in time with your music, and you've got a night to remember. The PA-220 is filled with plenty of extras too. Don't have a Bluetooth device to hand? There's an on-board FM radio or a USB slot for playing from memory sticks, so you'll never run out of listening options. Thanks to Class D amplification, this speaker is lightweight and portable without sacrificing durability. An X-Drive function helps to emphasise bass frequencies, ideal for larger or open-air spaces. For live performances, the PA-220 has dual inputs for microphones, with two wireless microphones straight out of the box. This makes the PA-220 ideal for karaoke parties, with Bluetooth connectivity allowing you to stream karaoke tracks directly to your speaker.
