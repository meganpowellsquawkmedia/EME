This Hotpoint integrated Dishwasher features: full size dishwasher with an outstanding number of available place settings. Exceptional, reduced water consumption. Black colour. Adjustable feet, for perfect stability on uneven floors and surfaces.

Half Load

The half load option only uses the required amount of energy and water without compromising on performance – designed for when the machine isn't full.

14 place setting

Load up to 14 place settings.

Inverter Motor

Thanks to its capacity to vary the spinning speed, the innovative Inverter Motor can properly calibrate water pressure and, therefore, cleaning force, in combination with an optimised electronic control at all times. The magnets allow a precise control of sprayers, directing the water with the right pressure, in the right spot and at the right time.

Flexiload

Flexibility makes space for everything - create additional space in the lower basket by loading pans vertically.

Delay Timer

The function to delay by up to 24 hours the start of the washing program, allowing you to run the dishwasher at any time of the day, even when you're out.

Tablet

It maximizes the efficiency of the detergent multi-use tabs.

Rack lift up

The adjustable 2nd rack can be moved up or down to create additional space to fit taller or bulky items.

E Energy class

At energy class E, this appliance delivers high performance, while always operating in an environmentally-friendly manner.

Hot water connection

Possibility to connect water to an hot water pipe (up to 60oC).

Overflow

Passive water protection system which detects leakages in the tub and prevents flooding in the house. In case of rupture of the tub, the water leaks into the lower compartment of the machine where the floating device triggers the fault alarm. Drain pump is activated and the tub is emptied.

Intensive

A dedicated program for heavily soiled crockery, with burnt food residuals, especially suitable for pans and saucepans.
