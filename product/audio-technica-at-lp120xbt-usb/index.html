This version of our critically-acclaimed AT-LP120X USB, now features full wireless Bluetooth® 5.0 connectivity, allowing you to enjoy your favourite vinyl records on the latest Bluetooth® speakers and wireless headphone technology.

 

 

Taking Your Vinyl to the Next Level

Crystal Clear Bluetooth® Wireless Audio

Compatible with most Bluetooth® speakers and headphones, the LP120XBT-USB pairs seamlessly to your devices. Simply press the Bluetooth® button, located next to the tonearm, for 4 seconds and connect to your chosen speakers, it’s that simple.

Plug and Play

The LP120XBT-USB is designed to give you a high-quality listening experience that’s easy to enjoy right out of the box. A built-in preamp means the LP120XBT-USB can be plugged directly into your existing speakers, or, with the simple press of a switch, connect wirelessly via Bluetooth® 5.0 to any bluetooth compatible speaker, supported home assistant or wireless headphones. Space saving with no external amps, no clutter and no mess; just incredible sound and reliable features designed with over half a century of Japanese audio innovation.

The LP120XBT-USB can also be plugged directly into your computer, for audio playback via your PC speakers or for simple and easy backup and transfer of your vinyl records to playback on your smart devices when on-the-go.
