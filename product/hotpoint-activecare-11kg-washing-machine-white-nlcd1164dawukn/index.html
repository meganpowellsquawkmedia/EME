Product description

For an exceptional stain-busting, intuitive laundry solution that protects and cares for your clothes, the Hotpoint ActiveCare NLLCD 1165 WD ADW UK N Washing Machine shares an incredible 1600rpm spin speed and wealth of programs to simplify your life. Best-in-class for stain removal, our innovative ActiveCare technology takes care of your clothes by removing more than 100 stains at just 20°C, so you can wear your favourite clothes time and time again. Offering the ultimate care and protection to your clothes with its Steam Hygiene Cycle that injects steam directly into the drum removing up to 99.9% of the most common bacteria without the use of chemical additives. Furthermore, this washer comes with the Allergy UK’s Seal of Approval as it removes 99.9% of major allergens, including dust mites and pollen, thanks to a high-temperature technology and extra rinses. this includes a reduction of bacteria build-up by 99.99% in your washing machine’s door seal thanks to antimicrobial protection, so your machine stays perfectly clean.
