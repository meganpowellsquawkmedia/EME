Highlights

ExtraDry: extra drying option for difficult-to-dry loads.

Activating the Extra Dry option dries your dishes extra-thoroughly by using a higher temperature during the rinse cycle and a longer drying phase – with just one push of a button. Extra Dry your dishes for those difficult-to-dry loads.

 

InfoLight – all the information you need.

A red led spot on the floor updates on whether your dishwasher is still operating or has finished the cycle.

 

Extra silence whenever you need it

Sometimes you need an extra silent environment at home to focus on your daily chores. The Silence on demand function can be activated via the Home Connect App and silences your dishwasher whenever necessary. It reduces noise to a minimum for up to 30 minutes at any time by an active pause while the dishwasher keeps on running. With the Silence on demand function you can focus on what matters most.

 

Automatic programmes ensure automatically the best cleaning performance.

Water usage, water temperature and rinse time are adjusted exactly to suit the level of soiling. This provides best results while saving water and energy.

 

Home Connect: an easy solution to help you get the most out of your appliance, with flexible control and additional features.

Personalise your dishwasher with up to 10 additional programs with Program Download. Whether you are looking for an intensive cleaning cycle for your dirty dishes, or a silent program for your open plan kitchen, it’s even easier to find an option that meets your needs. You can also use the Smart Finish Replenishment service to make sure you never run out of dishwasher tabs.

 

No more dishwasher leaks.

Leak protection is essential for dishwashers. Our Aqua Stop system delivers fail-safe leak protection. When the sensor detects a leak, the dishwasher automatically stops operating and pumps out water through a double-walled hose. With AquaStop you’re protected from water damage – and it’s valid for the lifetime of your appliance. *Please find warranty terms under www.bosch-home.ie/customer-service/care-protection-and-parts/additional-warranties#tab-navigation

 

Your Favourite function with just one touch

Make the most out of your dishwasher by simplying your daily life. With the Favourite function you can simply set up your most used programs and options as your favourite combination. The programmed setting can be started via the Home Connect app (available on selected models) or directly on the display with one step. Combine for example the Extra Dry option to the Eco 50° Program and start the dishwasher with only one touch. Personalized settings that get your dishes done by just one touch.

 

Download programmes for a perfect match to your everyday life.

Sometimes, it can be tough to find a dishwasher programme that suits your individual needs. Bosch dishwashers with Programme Download offer the possibility to download additional programmes via the Home Connect app. Programme Download ensures perfect dishwashing results thanks to programmes that fit your specific needs.

 

Smart programme selection

Choosing the right programme or each load can be tricky. Programme Assistant makes it easy. Simply answer a few questions in the Home Connect app about the type of load, the level of soiling and personal preferences for energy efficiency, dryness, speed and noise level, and the system recommends the right programme. Then just push start and look forward to your perfectly clean dishware, with minimum effort.

 

Remote support in case of an error

It can be annoying when appliances do not work the way they should. Remote Diagnostics is here to help. Simply call Bosch customer service, and with your permission, we can remotely screen your appliance, locate the issue and give you clear advice on how to solve it. In the event an issue cannot be solved, a Bosch service technician can come to your home to resolve the problem, equipped with all the necessary parts already to hand. Smart support, smart solutions.
