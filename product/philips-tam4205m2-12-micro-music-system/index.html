Description Big sound for the music you love Streaming, FM radio, CDs and more! This compact micro system with bookshelf-style speakers fills larger rooms with rich, 80 W sound. Plus, support for Auracast™ lets you broadcast what you're playing to compatible speakers around the house. Powerful Stereo Sound for Everything you Love Get lost in music! Bookshelf-style 2-way speakers give you rich, immersive stereo sound from 4" woofers, 0.75" tweeters and bass-reflex ports. The system's powerful 80 W (RMS) output lets you pump up the volume for a party or a larger room. Perfect for big lounges and open-plan living spaces. CD Classics, Radio, Streaming and More Music, podcasts, news, dramas, sports—the list is endless! You can stream your sounds via Bluetooth®, tune in to FM radio, or connect to other sources like flash drives via USB or turntables via audio-in. Bluetooth® 5.4 and Auracast™ Streaming at Home Support for Auracast™ lets you broadcast the sound from this micro system to multiple Auracast™ compatible speakers around the house. Plus, Bluetooth® LE Audio and the LC3 codec are supported so you'll enjoy noticeably better sound when streaming from your smart device to the micro system. Philips Entertainment app. Simple Sound Control Got wide-ranging tastes? You won't even have to get out of your chair to get the best sound for what you're listening to!
