Description A sleep trainer which teaches you child day and night. Sometimes children find it difficult to judge when it is day and when it is night. They wake up and think it's time to get up when they should be in bed for a few more hours. This problem often becomes worse as the days get longer and shorter or when summer and winter time begin. The BC-100TIGER is a sleep trainer that helps children develop a sense of time. How exactly does the sleep trainer work? As a mum or dad, you set the sleep trainer to a time that you find suitable for getting up. You put the sleep trainer in your child's bedroom in a place where he or she can see it well. The display has a blue colour at night and shows a moon, this is a sign for your child to stay in bed. Has the time you set been reached? Then the display changes to an orange screen in which a sun can be seen. Time to get up! This way you can train any child, even if they can't tell the time, to stay in bed longer ensuring a better night’s sleep for you. The sleep trainer is wireless and rechargeable. We supply a micro-USB cable for easy charging. The brightness of the screen can be adjusted in 7 positions. You can also use it as a night light. The BC-100TIGER can also be used as an alarm clock, with alarm.
