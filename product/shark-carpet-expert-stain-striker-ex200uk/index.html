Shark CarpetXpert Deep Carpet Cleaner with Built-In StainStriker | EX200UK

Keep your home sparkling from carpet to rugs with the Shark CarpetXpert Deep Carpet Cleaner with StainStriker! The CarpetXpert combines a powerful deep carpet cleaner with a built-in stain and spot cleaner for a supercharged stain elimination.

DEEP CARPET CLEANER

Built on decades of cleaning innovations, experience Shark’s powerful suction and floor cleaning expertise – now enhanced with carpet washing and stain removal technology. CarpetXpert delivers an 8x deeper clean than a regular vacuum – the high-pressure spray penetrates deep into carpet fibres and evenly distributes a dual-action cleaning solution, without oversaturating your carpet, removing layers of dirt and grime. The high-speed brush-roll maintains contact with your carpet at every turn – 6 rows of uninterrupted bristles leave no cleaning gaps where dirt may be missed.

FAST DRY TIME 

Shark’s powerful suction extracts dirt and liquid for fast drying, leaving carpets clean and revived, and instantly eliminating odours. Finish your clean with Dry Only mode to dry with suction, without dispensing water or cleaning solution.

BUILT-IN STAINSTRIKER SPOT REMOVER

Instantly eliminate tough stains anywhere in your home with the built-in StainStriker spot cleaner, for the best stubborn stain removal. With a long-reach, flexible hose and interchangeable attachments, it’s perfect for tackling stains on pet beds, rugs, carpets, stairs, sofas, upholstery, car interiors and more.

20X MORE STAIN-STRIKING POWER

No need to pre-treat stains with Shark’s dual-activated cleaning solution. Two bespoke formulas mix and activate inside the handheld StainStriker tool to double down on stains and odours. The CarpetXpert Deep Clean Pro formula removes layers of ground-in dirt and grime and the StainStriker Oxy Multiplier formula lifts and extracts stubborn stains and odours, unleashing the power of OXY to eliminate tough stains. These two cleaning solutions mix and activate inside the handheld stain eliminator as you clean, targeting and removing even the toughest stains. Strike through pet stains, odours, embedded mud and dirt, spilled red wine or coffee, and more.

PET STAIN TRAPPING TOOL 

Purposefully designed for pet owners, this tool collects hair, debris and dirty liquid in a separate compartment for ultimate convenience and hygienic emptying, stopping larger debris and solids from entering the hose and main unit.

LARGE TOUGH STAIN TOOL 

Quickly clean large areas like stairs, sofas or mattresses with this wide scrubbing tool.

TOUGH STAIN REVERSIBLE BRISTLE TOOL 

Strike through everyday dirt, spots and stains with this versatile tool, featuring multi-purpose reversible bristles for different surfaces.

INTEGRATED CREVICE TOOL 

Perfect for cleaning hard-to-reach places such as in between sofa cushions, car seats or around pet beds.

HOSE-CLEANING TOOL

Cleaning the extendable hose from the inside, this handy tool makes it easy to keep your StainStriker clean after each use.
