Description About Swissvoice Because staying in touch and maintain the social and intergenerational link has never been more important, Swissvoice offers a wide range of phones for seniors and people experiencing a sensory deficiency (sight, hearing, dexterity, mobility). Easy-to-use, innovative and high-quality, our solutions are designed to simplify the user experience, and bring people the extra help they are searching for. Our products include home and mobile phones with exclusive Swissvoice functions to reassure user as well as family helper. About Swissvoice Xtra 2355 Swissvoice Xtra 2355 is a unique combination of characteristics and features to overcome sensory deficiencies, in a robust cordless phone ensuring mobility during your conversations. From dexterity side, its big separated raised keys for ease of dial and read and extra-large ergonomic handset shape make it easy for unskilled hands. The stable base ensures a secured hang-up. To support failing eyesight, the phone features a hands-free base with photo memories for instant-dialling of your favourite contacts and a super large easy-to-read display with big letters and digits. Calls can be answered from the base even if the handset has been left in another room. All sound levels can easily be adjusted by dedicated keys: a +40dB audio-boost ensures effortless conversations. The extra loud ringers adjustable to up to 90dB for the loudest ones, with large ringing indicators on base and handset make sure to never miss a call and avoid unnecessary move, the four direct memories for the favourite contacts are equipped with caller announcement to recognise the calls from the nearest. Last, the answering-machine is equipped with slow playback of message to have time to note a name or a phone number. Swissvoice Xtra 2355 is really made to assist seniors or anyone with hearing, sight or dexterity challenges. Reassuring Base Station The four direct memory buttons with pictures on the base make it so easy and safe to call the favourite contacts, closest family or even the doctor, directly in hands-free mode, at the press of a button. And if the phone rings and the handset has been left in another room, no more panic: thanks to the hands-free key with backlight, it is so easy to answer any call. Easy-to-Read Large Display Xtra 2355 is equipped with a super-large three-line backlit display with big letters and numbers to support those with sight challenges. It is so easy to read who is calling, check the dialled number or facilitate the use of the menu. Slow Playback of Messages The 30-minute answering machine with five keys on base for ease-of-use, is equipped with a very special slow playback message function. Once activated, this feature allows to slow down the message to clearly understand what is said and have the time to write a name or a phone number. It is so convenient! Call-Block Function Call-Block function to avoid nuisance or commercial calls bringing anxiety to people staying at home and protect your parent's privacy. Two easy-to-program modes: Manual mode to easily block up to 100 numbers during or after the calls at a press on the dedicated key. Automatic mode with on and off, to only allow stored or VIP numbers to ring.
