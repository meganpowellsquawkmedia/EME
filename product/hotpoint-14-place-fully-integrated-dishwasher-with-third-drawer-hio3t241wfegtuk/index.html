Hotpoint 14 Place Fully Integrated Dishwasher With Third Drawer - HIO3T241WFEGTUK

 

Get tidied up after dinner with this Hotpoint dishwasher. It can hold 14 place settings at once, making it the perfect addition to any medium-sized household. It also has a quick wash, and this will get your plates and bowls spotless again in just 30 minutes. If you’ve treated the family to your famous lasagne recipe, just pop the dish into the 3D Zone Wash area, and any burnt on splatters of sauce will be blasted off by a powerful water stream. When the cycle finishes, the door will automatically open by up to 10cm to let in a natural flow of air, not only drying pots hygienically, but it’s much cheaper and environmentally friendly too.
FEATURES:
SILENCE 40DB:

An even quite programme with a reduced noise, allowing to use the dishwasher at night.

 

INTENSIVE:

A dedicated program for heavily soiled crockery, with burnt food residuals, especially suitable for pans and saucepans.

 

TABLET:

It maximizes the efficiency of the detergent multi-use tabs.

ACTIVEDRY:

ActiveDry System automatically opens the dishwasher door at the end of the washing cycle to deliver better drying performance and improved energy efficiency.

 

STAINLESS STEEL INTERIOR:

A long lasting stainless steel tough interior that protects and cleans.

 

DELAY TIMER:

The function to delay by up to 24 hours the start of the washing program, allowing you to run the dishwasher at any time of the day, even when you're out.

 

GLASS CARE:

Keep your favourite glassware free from water marks wash after wash with a dedicated cycle and specially designed glass holders that ensure your glassware is safely washed and dried to perfection. Simply fold away when not in use to maximise your space.

 

INVERTER MOTOR:

Thanks to its capacity to vary the spinning speed, the innovative Inverter Motor can properly calibrate water pressure and, therefore, cleaning force, in combination with an optimised electronic control at all times. The magnets allow a precise control of sprayers, directing the water with the right pressure, in the right spot and at the right time.

 

FLEXILOAD:

Capacity for up to 13 place settings is created by a flexible storage system, including a Vertical Zone that lets you secure pans vertically to free up space in the lower basket.

3D ZONE WASH:

The combined action of the powerful water jets from the upper and lower spray arms and the variable speed inverter motor, makes sure precisely the right amount of water is used throughout the selected basket for either 40% more cleaning power* or 40% more energy efficiency*, depending on your needs. *Average percentage of performance increase vs a non-3D Zone Wash appliance running Intensive cycle, obtained on 17 different items with different typology of dirt. Test results verified by VDE. ** Verified in the Report N° 236127-AS6-1 by VDE Test Institute.

 

 

ENERGY CLASS C:

Superior performances coupled with high energy savings are guaranteed at C energy class. So you can give the best care for your home and the ennvironment.
