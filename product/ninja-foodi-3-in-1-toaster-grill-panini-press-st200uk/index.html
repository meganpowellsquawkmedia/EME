Ninja Foodi 3-in-1 Toaster, Grill & Panini Press | ST200UK

More than a toaster. From perfectly golden toast, bagels and crumpets to pastries, paninis and toasted sandwiches, this 3-in-1 appliance quickly and easily creates meals and snacks for the whole household.

Get creative with 7 customisable cooking functions – Toast, Bagel, Defrost, Grill, Panini, Bake and Reheat.

Create meals and snacks in minutes

Enjoy even, edge-to-edge toasting to your perfect shade. Not just for breakfast, grill artisan bread loaded with toppings, cheese and ham croissants, brunch muffins, pizza bagels, quesadillas, even fish finger sandwiches and loaded potato waffles. Press café-style paninis, grilled cheese sandwiches and toasties filled with all your favourite ingredients using the included panini press accessory. You can even reheat pizza slices to restore that fresh-out-of-the-oven finish.

Flip to transform from toaster to grill

With a unique flip design, you can choose between two cooking positions.

Toast your favourite bread, bagels and crumpets to perfection when positioned as a vertical slot toaster or flip down to transform into a versatile horizontal grill to bake, grill and more.

There’s no need to worry about mess with Total Crumb Control, featuring a uniquely designed crumb tray to catch crumbs from both cooking positions.

Toaster mode

  • The uniquely-designed slot and heating elements deliver even, edge-to-edge results
  • The long, wide slot easily fits artisan bread or 2 bagel halves
  • Everyone can choose their ideal toast shade with 7 different toast shade settings
  • Master the art of the perfectly toasted bagel – the Bagel setting automatically toasts bagel halves to perfection using a higher temperature on the cut side for a golden outside and soft inside
  • The Defrost setting toasts frozen bread in a matter of minutes

Grill mode

  • Flip down to grill classic cheese-on-toast, crumpets and muffins loaded with toppings, cheese-topped garlic bread, delicious toasted sandwiches and crispy filled paninis with a golden finish – from mozzarella, pesto and tomato to tasty tuna melts.
  • Easily bake everyone’s favourite treats for breakfast and beyond, from buttery croissants to flaky pastries.
  • 3 temperature settings give you total control
  • The included Bake Tray converts the toaster slot into a closed oven
  • The clever Panini Press accessory makes it easy to create perfectly toasted paninis with crispy griddle markings
  • Want to sneak a peek? During cooking you can remove the Bake Tray to check on your food. The timer will pause until you reinsert the tray.
  • Reheat leftovers such as pizza slices to restore that fresh-out-of-the-oven finish

Enjoy endless possibilities at the touch of a button, thanks to the easy-to-use digital control panel and built-in timer.

With its contemporary matte design, this sleek black toaster makes a stylish addition to any kitchen.

Inspiration is included in the box with a Chef-Created Recipe Guide to get you started.
