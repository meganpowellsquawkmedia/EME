Description The Classic Phono (by Lenco) TCD-2551WD is an all-in-one multifunctional music system with a stunning retro design that enhances any interior. The TCD-2551WD is characterized by the built-in record player with belt drive but offers so much more. Effortlessly blending classic functionalities with modern playback options, the wooden retro record player features Bluetooth® reception, AM/FM radio, a built-in CD player, and a cassette player. This provides a complete listening experience with the perfect balance between the nostalgic charm of vintage aesthetics and modern functionality. The TCD-2551WD has an MDF housing and comes with a remote control for added convenience. Belt-drive Record Player Thanks to the reliable belt drive, a technology known for its smooth and low-vibration operation, the record player delivers clear and precise playback of your beloved records. The record player supports playing records at three speeds: 33, 45, or 78 revolutions per minute. Using the metal hinge, you can position the lid of the retro record player at a 45° angle, allowing you to play your records with both an open and closed cabinet. Additionally, you can convert your favourite tracks to MP3 with the built-in USB encoding. Various Playback Options The Classic Phono TCD-2551WD is versatile in its use. In addition to playing your favourite records, you can easily stream playlists from your smartphone or tablet using Bluetooth 5.0. Moreover, play your CDs with the built-in CD player and tune in to your favourite radio stations with the integrated AM/FM radio. Using the analogue tuner at the front of the TCD-2551WD, a classic element in radio technology, you can manually navigate through radio frequencies. The audio system also allows you to revisit the nostalgia of cassette tapes. Keep control of all your playback settings with the clear LCD screen. In addition to converting vinyl to MP3, it is possible to convert CD, Bluetooth, radio, and line-in to MP3 and save it on a USB stick. Two built-in Speakers Enjoy a clear, warm, and high-quality sound with the two built-in speakers, each with a power of 5 Watts RMS. Whether you're savouring the warm tones of vinyl, the refined sounds of a CD, or the nostalgia of a cassette tape, the speakers offer rich sound reproduction. Classic Phono by Lenco Discover the nostalgic charm of Classic Phono record players, a proud representation of over seventy-five years of Lenco's expertise in Hi-Fi and consumer electronics. The innovation and reliability that define Lenco are reflected in the warm tones and enduring performance of our Classic Phono record players with a timeless retro design. Experience your music in an authentic and stylish manner, supported by the proven quality of Lenco. Classic Phono, where the past and present come together.
