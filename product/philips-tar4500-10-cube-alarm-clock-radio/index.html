Description Wake Up Ready to Go Wake up gently, go to sleep more easily! With a big, clear clock, gently rising dual alarms, a handy USB-C port and one-touch daylight-saving time adjustment, this FM clock radio will keep anyone in the family on track all year round. Large, Clear Display with Big Clock Digits The easy-to-read display boasts big clock digits so you can clearly see what time it is, and you can adjust the brightness to suit your room or dim the display at night. To adjust the time for daylight saving, simply press the dedicated DST button on top of the clock. USB-C Port to Charge Your Phone A handy USB-C port lets you use the clock radio's power supply to charge your phone at night. If you have a lamp plugged into one bedside power socket and your clock radio plugged into another, you won't need to hunt for extra sockets! Simple Controls. No Complicated Menus There are buttons for key controls on top of this clock radio. You won't have to scroll through menus—simply push the relevant buttons to set or snooze the alarms, adjust the display brightness, tune the radio, change the volume or show daylight saving time. Dual Alarm Function. Set Two Alarms You can set two alarms—perfect if you're likely to snooze through the first alarm, and great for siblings or partners who share a room but have different wake-up times. Both alarms can be set to use either an alarm tone or the radio, and the volume starts low and gradually increases. No more waking up with a shock! FM Digital Tuner with up to 20 Presets Classical, jazz, the latest hits or the news? Whatever you love to listen to, the digital radio tuner delivers crystal-clear reception. You can also set up to 20 presets so that you can go straight to your favourite stations. Sleep Timer for Restful Bedtime Routines The Sleep Timer lets you drift off to sleep with the radio playing in the background. After your preset time has elapsed, the radio turns off automatically. Simply press the sleep button on top of the clock radio to set the duration of the Sleep Timer (or turn it off). Battery Backup for the Clock and Alarms This alarm-clock radio has a battery backup function which keeps the clock and alarms running in the event of a power failure. Insert 2 x AAA batteries (not included) into the battery compartment: if the power fails during the night, you won't need to reset the clock.
