Description Introducing the Lenco LS-470WA, an all-in-one turntable from Lenco's Expertise line with a stunning, robust design in the elegant walnut colour. The LS-470WA is a turntable with built-in speakers, belt drive, and Bluetooth®, featuring a high-quality Audio Technica cartridge. The detachable dust cover protects your turntable from dust, while the unique design and elegant dark wood finish add a touch of class to the immediate surroundings of the turntable. Built-in Speakers (80W) The four built-in speakers, two of 30W and two of 10W, deliver a powerful and balanced sound. This allows you to fully enjoy the warm tones of vinyl, but thanks to Bluetooth compatibility, the LS-470WA also provides the option to enjoy your favourite music from devices like your smartphone, tablet, or laptop. Exceptional Sound Reproduction with Belt Drive Further enhancing the sound reproduction of the LS-470WA is the reliable belt drive of the turntable. Belt drive, known for its smooth and low-vibration operation, ensures a clear and accurate playback of your beloved records. The turntable supports playing records at two speeds: 33 and 45 revolutions per minute. The elastic belt ensures a smooth and consistent rotation, resulting in a versatile turntable effortlessly switching between both speeds to meet the modern needs of audio enthusiasts. Moving Magnetic Cartridge The turntable comes with an Audio Technica AT-3600 Moving Magnetic Cartridge (MMC). This element, containing the needle that tracks the grooves of your vinyl records, ensures that each note comes to life with precision. Simple Operation, Despite Various Features Despite its extensive features, operating the LS-470WA remains effortless and intuitive. Power your turntable and immediately enjoy your favourite vinyl records. The start-stop button allows you to stop the record at any time and restart it where you left off. Additionally, the LS-470WA features an RCA output and an AUX input, and the turntable enables encoding your favourite tracks in mp3 format via the included USB cable. All of this is designed to delight the eyes and ears of the most enthusiastic music lovers.
