Clean Edges Like Never Before with ScrubExtend. - Introducing ScrubExtend, the industry’s most durable and high-performance swing-arm mopping system.

Cut Through Hair Wrap with FlexiRazor™ - Say goodbye to pesky hair tangling thanks to J15 Ultra's all-new, SGS-certified FlexiRazor™ design. Make tangled hair a non-issue with Eureka‘s exclusive FlexiRazor™ technology. Unlike most anti-tangle roller brushes in the industry, J15 Ultra utilizes active cutting rather than stationary blades to slice through hair wrap and keep your robot cleaning at peak efficiency. High-density blades, operating 400 times per minute with a 10 cm reciprocating vibration, cover the entire brush to remove wrapped hair. FlexiRazor™ has an anti-tangle effect.

Manual detangling a thing of the past. Roller Brush and Both Ends with Less Tangling - Eureka J15 Ultra optimizes both ends of the roller brush by improving the bristles to prevent hair from winding around the ends. This reduces the hair tangling rate there by 55%*, further reducing the need for manual maintenance and ensuring smoother cleaning operation.

All-In-One Base Station - Automate the routine maintenance of your robot vacuum with J15 Ultra‘s advanced base station. Automatic mop cleaning and drying: Clean the mop in hot water to break up stubborn stains and dry completely, preventing odors and ensuring the J15 Ultra is ready for the next clean. Self-Emptying: The base station can automatically collect dust, and the built-in 3 L large-capacity dust bag provides up to 75 days* of use.

Hot water mop-washing ensures stains are effectively broken down, greatly improving cleaning efficiency. When J15 Ultra returns to the base station, the mop-washing system automatically cleans the mop with 75 ℃ hot water, penetrating deep into the brush bristles to quickly and efficiently dissolve stubborn stains like grease. After the mop is washed with hot water, it is then automatically dried with 55 ℃ hot air. Circulating hot air throughout the base station, the mop, wastewater pipe, base, filter, etc., are thoroughly dried, preventing odour formation. The 3 L large-capacity dust bag in the station delivers up to 75 days* of use before needing to replace the dust bag to keep your cleaning hands-free for longer.

WhisperWork™ Dock - Enjoy quieter base station operation with advanced WhisperWork™ technology, ideal for households with pets or children. Employing seven layers of noise reduction, including noise reduction at the motor source, vibration damping along the transmission path, and flow path optimization, the base station of J15 Ultra keeps self-emptying at low noise levels.

A Good Helper for Carpet Cleaning. - The FlexiRise™ driven mop supports a lift of up to 12 mm, in line with the carpet-first strategy to avoid wetting the carpet.The finest carpet cleaning performance is accomplished by deep cleaning and a powerful suction force of 19,000 Pa.

Powerful Suction Comparable to a Canister Vacuum - Give your home a deeper clean with jaw-dropping suction power. Our self-developed fan structure, made of aluminum—which has better thermal conductivity than traditional plastic fans—improves heat dissipation and maximizes motor power. This design enables a maximum suction of 19,000 Pa. Thoroughly clean crevices or give carpets a deep vacuuming with a household debris removal rate of 99%, which can even compete with canister vacuums.

Say Goodbye to Damp Carpet - Eureka J15 Ultra accurately identifies carpets. A common issue with robot vacuums is that the mop cannot lift, causing carpets to get wet and potentially damaged. Powered by FlexiRise™, the mop on the Eureka J15 Ultra automatically lifts when moving onto a carpet or rug, effectively preventing them from getting wet.

Carpet Priority Mode - If the user‘s carpet thickness exceeds 12 mm, the user can set it in the app to have the robot vacuum cleaner first vacuum the carpet and then avoid dragging the carpet to further ensure that the carpet is not wetted by the mop.
