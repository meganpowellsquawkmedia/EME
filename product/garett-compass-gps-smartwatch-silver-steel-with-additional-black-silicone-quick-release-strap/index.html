Description Garett Compass – A Reliable Outdoor Smartwatch The Compass features an AMOLED screen protected by Gorilla Glass and a precise GPS module. It's a durable sports watch that's suitable for both city life and extreme adventures. Two Styles, One Smartwatch The Garett Compass is available in two versions: with a silver case and orange strap, or with a black case and green strap. Each set includes comfortable black silicone straps that combine durability with comfort. AMOLED Screen with Gorilla Glass Protection The 1.43″ AMOLED display with a resolution of 466 × 466 pixels provides a clear and fluid image. The Always On Display feature allows you to constantly view information, while Gorilla Glass protects the screen from scratches and damage. Reliable Satellite Navigation The Garett Compass smartwatch is equipped with a precise GPS module that supports six satellite systems: GPS, GLONASS, Beidou, Galileo, NAVIC, and QZSS. Thanks to broad signal compatibility, the device ensures accurate location and stable operation even in hard-to-reach areas where other devices often lose coverage. Reliable Operation in all Conditions The Garett Compass smartwatch is designed to be highly resistant to extreme outdoor conditions. The device easily withstands shock, moisture, dust, and extreme temperatures, remaining a reliable companion both in everyday use and during demanding off-road expeditions. Complete Freedom of Activity The Garett Compass is a smartwatch designed for activity in all conditions. 5 ATM water resistance allows you to use the watch while swimming, intense workouts in the rain, or hiking. Over 100 sport modes, including running, cycling, water sports, trekking, and strength training, provide comprehensive support for a variety of activities. Easy Connections and Operation The Garett Compass is equipped with a microphone and speaker that allow you to make calls via Bluetooth directly from your wrist. A built-in voice assistant makes it easy to control smartwatch and smartphone functions, ensuring comfort even during intense activity. Notifications and Music at your Fingertips The Garett Compass lets you control music playback directly from your wrist, and also notifies you of incoming messages, emails, and app notifications. These features make it easy to stay connected and provide multimedia access both on a daily basis and during travel or outdoor activities. Full Support for Health The Garett Compass smartwatch allows you to monitor your heart rate, blood pressure, and oxygen saturation (SpO₂), providing detailed data on your body's condition. Sleep analysis and respiratory training support the recovery process and help you maintain the right balance between exercise and rest. Works with the Garett Fit Pro app Garett Compass works seamlessly with Android and iOS systems, using the dedicated Garett Fit Pro app. It enables secure data collection, intuitive feature management, and quick access to health statistics, training, and activity data in one place.
