Description More and more people today suffer from a deterioration of their hearing, sometimes starting at a young age. With almost 15 years of experience, amplicomms is accompanying this development with a wide range of quality products adapted to different hearing needs. Our expertise allows us to offer you products with loud yet clear sound for effortless phone calls. Amplified Audio with Unique Sound Ideal for people with hearing impairment and for all users looking for a phone with amplified sound. The ringtones can be set up to 90 dB so you never miss a call. The volume can be set to five different levels, both for the earpiece and in hands-free mode, to suit the user's hearing ability. The audio boost button (+10dB) can also be used during the call. Extra Audio Boost Key Thanks to the audio boost key, ideally placed on the side of the handset, you can increase the volume during a call without having to put the handset down to adjust the volume. Practical especially in noisy environments: The maximum amplification is +35 dB. Best Phone for Hard of Hearing - This home telephone has an adjustable ringer volume that can be increased up to 90 db. This is louder than a power lawnmower! The phone also includes a visual ring indicator, making incoming calls even harder to miss! Direct Memory Buttons - The BigTel 1500 home telephone included 3 simple to use direct memory buttons. This allows elderly relatives or friends to simply call their nearest and dearest at the click of a button. Big Buttons - Memory buttons and dialling keys are all extra-large allowing them to be easily seen. Ideal for the visually impaired or the unsteady hands of any older relatives of friends in need of a bit of extra help. Increased Audio Volume - When on a call the audio can be increased by 35dB. making conversations easier to hear and follow! The phone also has a built in hands free function. Large Character Display - The large character backlit display allows incoming calls to be easily seen. Combined with the extra-large buttons, making a call is also easier than ever for the hard of sight. Additional Handset Included - The BigTel 1502 system comes with an additional handset, allowing a phone can be also placed by a bed or in another room to the main phone. Helping to reduce the chance of missing a call and meaning a phone is never too far away if needed. Base station with handset, and an additional, easy-to-install handset – Call forwarding from one handset to the other Three-party conference possible with two internal and one external call partner. Call Blocking - Block unwanted call with the dedicated call blocker button. Block up to 100 numbers so you never have to receive unwanted calls again.
