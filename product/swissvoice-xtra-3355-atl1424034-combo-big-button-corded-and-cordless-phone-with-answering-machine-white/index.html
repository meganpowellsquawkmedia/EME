Description Staying in touch and maintaining social and intergenerational connections has never been more important. Swissvoice offers a wide range of home and mobile phones for people with sensory or motor deficits. Our products are user-friendly, innovative and of high quality. They offer many assistive features for people with visual and hearing impairments, as well as for people with motor impairments. Swissvoice Xtra 3355 Combo: corded & cordless For seniors with impaired hearing or vision or motor impairments Large, Raised, Talking Keys make dialling easier. The extra-large, ergonomic shape of the handset makes the phone easy to hold, even for unsteady hands. The stable base ensures a secure hold when hanging up. The direct dial keys can be programmed with an announcement that is played back when the contact is called, as well as when an incoming call is received. The Xtra 3355 Combo is the successful combination of a corded telephone and a cordless handset. The Double Plus in Operating Comfort The Xtra 3355 Combo has four programmable direct memory keys with insertable pictures on the base station. These can be used to call the most important contacts directly in handsfree mode at the touch of a button, even without the handset. The same telephone numbers are automatically stored on the handset under the letters A-B-C-D. High Quality Display The Xtra 3355 has an extra-large, three-line display with pale blue backlighting. Large letters and numbers make it easier for people with impaired vision to recognise the number dialled or the caller. This also makes it much easier to operate the menu. Answering Machine with Comfort The answering machine integrated in the base of the Xtra 3355 has 30 minutes of recording time, and is easy to operate via five keys. The highlight: the built-in function for slow playback of messages! This makes it easier to understand what is being said and leaves time to make notes about the call. One Phonebook for All Don't waste time storing your favourite numbers multiple times! Create your phone numbers only once and share them with the other handsets. The synchronised phonebook has a capacity of 100 names and numbers. Call-Block Function Thanks to two different call-block functions, nuisance calls are a thing of the past: A manual mode to block up to 100 numbers during or after a call, and an automatic mode so that calls are only accepted from stored or VIP numbers. Versatile in Use All you need for the handset supplied is a power socket and the base to pair it with. Then you can place it on the bedside table or in the kitchen, for example. Calls can be forwarded to further handsets* and you can even make three-way calls with one external and two internal callers. Speaking Keys The photo keys correspond to the A - B - C - D key on the handset. They can be stored with an individual message. The announcement of the numbers on the numeric keypad corresponds to the set menu language. The menu is available in English, French, German, Italian, Dutch, Spanish and Portuguese. Easy Installation Up to four handsets can be coupled to the base stations of the Swissvoice Xtra 2355, Swissvoice Xtra 2355 Duo, Swissvoice Xtra 3355 Combo and Swissvoice Xtra 3355 Combo Duo. Additionally available Swissvoice Xtra handsets can be used to expand the base stations.
