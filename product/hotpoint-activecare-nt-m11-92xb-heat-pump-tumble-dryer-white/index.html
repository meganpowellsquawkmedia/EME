Dedicated to caring for the fabrics your love, the Hotpoint ActiveCare NT M11 92XB Tumble Dryer combines a family-sized 9kg capacity with programs devoted to prolonging the vibrancy in your clothes, and an A++ Energy Rating to save money. Our impressive Heat Pump Technology allows the moisture to be removed from your clothes and recycled through the machine, providing you with perfectly dry clothes. This technology is more of an economical approach to tumble drying, as it can save you up to 50% in energy consumption. The cooler temperature, alongside a gentle drum movement ensures delicate care for your clothes during the cycle, giving them the adequate treatment they deserve. Reduce wrinkles and make ironing easier than ever with Pre-Iron cycle. Thanks to the patented ActiveCare technology, the new Hotpoint tumble dryer reduces fabric wear out by up to 40%, minimising stress on your clothes, keeping them looking their best for longer. The clever Anti-Allergy option allows higher temperatures to remove bacteria and allergens from your laundry. Great for those that suffer from allergies or have sensitive skin.
