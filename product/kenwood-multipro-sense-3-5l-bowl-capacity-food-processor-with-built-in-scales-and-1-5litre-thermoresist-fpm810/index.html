Multipro Sense Food Processor FPM810

Featuring intelligent auto and variable speed functions, a heat resistant ThermoResist glass blender and revolutionary shatterproof TritanTM bowls.

Multipro Sense: The Intelligent Food Processor from Kenwood

More capable, durable and intelligent than any other Kenwood food processor on the market, the Multipro Sense comes with an incredible range of specially designed attachments. This clever food processor also features both a variable and an auto speed function that allows you to choose the best speed for each accessory. This means that you can focus on creating even more exciting and adventurous meals. If you can imagine it, Multipro Sense will help you to do it. Intelligently.

Cutting Edge Technology

The ultra-tough glass of the ThermoResist blender attachment, unique to the Multipro Sense, allows for both hot and cold blending without the risk of the material cracking or shattering.

In addition to this the Multipro Sense is the first food processor to use revolutionary Tritan™ plastic for its 3.5 litre main bowl and 1.5 litre mini bowl. This tough, durable, BPA (Bisphenol-A) free plastic is impact and shatter resistant and is designed to withstand extreme use and repeated dishwasher cleaning without losing its sparkling transparency.

Intelligence Built for Precision Results

This powerful and innovative food processor features a 1000W motor and patented Multipro dual drive system to deliver optimum performance and speed as both a processor and blender. The intelligent auto speed function provides the maximum speed for each attachment, while the eight speed settings and additional pulse option allow for total user control when necessary.

With integrated scales located on the base of the food processor (UK FPM810 model only), weighing ingredients straight into the bowl has never been easier. In addition, the Multipro Sense automatically switches to Eco mode if the machine is left idle for 30 minutes, effectively reducing energy consumption by up to 50 per cent.

Incredible Versatility

The innovative in-bowl drive allows greater capacity for your food preparation needs and makes cleaning easier, while the new 'scything' design of the slicing discs achieves precision-cut slices with less waste.
