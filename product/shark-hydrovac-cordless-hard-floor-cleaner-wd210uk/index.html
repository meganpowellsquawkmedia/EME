Shark HydroVac Cordless Hard Floor Cleaner | WD210UK

Introducing the 3-in-1 hard floor cleaner that vacuums, mops, and cleans itself. With cordless freedom, clean the average UK home in one charge*.

Dropped an egg on the kitchen floor? Spilled a bowl of cereal and milk? No need to worry. Shark HydroVac vacuums and mops at the same time, pulling in wet messes, dirt, debris and grime to leave a clean surface that you can walk on almost immediately.

Perfect for everyday deep cleaning and unexpected messes alike, it’s ideal for homes with children, pets and other mess-makers. Instantly tackle dropped drinks in the kitchen, playroom paint spills, hallway mud and pawprints, food spills, and so much more.

What is HydroVac
HydroVac combines powerful suction and hydro mopping, at the same time, with two cleaning modes:

Hard Floor Mode

  • Deep cleans hard floors
  • Eliminates spills and dirt, and removes odours – clean up everything from everyday grime and muddy footprints to spilled drinks, cereal and other wet messes
  • Perfect for all sealed hard floors including hardwood, tile, marble and sealed laminate

Freshen Mode

  • Cleans and refreshes area rugs, thin-pile rugs and floor mats – perfect for door mats
  • Removes surface dirt and odours

Floors dry ultra-fast
Walk on floors and rugs almost immediately after cleaning.

Odour Neutraliser Technology
Shark’s multi-surface floor cleaning solution with Odour Neutraliser Technology helps to eliminate stubborn odours on floors and area rugs, and helps to prevent odours from reforming, for a fresher-smelling home. It dries quickly and leaves no residue.

Cleaning solution refills are available to purchase from Shark.

Antimicrobial brush-roll** starts clean and stays clean
HydroVac Technology helps to keep the brush-roll clean and odour-free.
Unlike traditional untreated brush-rolls, Shark’s antimicrobial brush-roll** helps to prevent odour and bacterial growth between uses. The continuous self-cleaning system helps prevent dirt from being redeposited back on your floor.

Easy to empty & cleans itself
An efficient two-tank system keeps clean and dirty water separate. Need to empty mid-clean? Debris filtration technology in the dirty water tank keeps solids separate, so you can quickly dispose of dirty water with Express Empty.
When you’ve finished cleaning, simply place your HydroVac on the dock and run Self-Cleaning mode – the brush-roll, floorhead and interior tubing will be thoroughly washed and dried in just minutes, ready for the next use. Charging while on the dock, this cordless cleaner is always ready to go when you need it.

Simple controls
Select a floor type using the convenient handle controls. The LED display shows real-time cleaning information – view your remaining battery life, surface type and cleaning solution level at a glance.

Sleek & stylish
Boasting looks as powerful as its cleaning performance, this cordless cleaner is styled with a slim silhouette and polished metallic trims. LED headlights illuminate hidden debris.

Shark offers a free 3-year guarantee for your peace of mind.

Please note, this product is not suitable for use on plush/thick area rugs or carpet.
