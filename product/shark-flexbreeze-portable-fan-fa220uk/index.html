Shark FlexBreeze Portable Fan|FA220UK

Stay fresh and cool with the FlexBreeze for Shark. This hybrid indoor and outdoor fan can transform from pedestal to tabletop, corded to cordless and comes with a InstaCool Misting attachment for a refreshing mist outdoors.

Features

  • Hybrid Indoor & Outdoor Fan - A single button transforms FlexBreeze into a corded tabletop, floor, or pedestal fan. Go cordless for a refreshing breeze on your patio or deck.
  • Up To 20 Hours of Battery Runtime - Enjoy long-lasting cordless power outdoors.
  • Customise Your Cool - 5 cooling fan speeds, 180° oscillation, 55° tilt and a controller.
  • InstaCool Misting Attachment - Attach it to your garden hose pipe for a refreshing mist.
  • Easy to Use - 3 simple button controls on the fan allow you to select your perfect fan settings.
  • Stay Cool - Cools indoor spaces by up to 5 °C
  • Battery Indicator – See how full the battery is charged with 5 distinct colours and light patterns. The fan even works when it’s charging.
