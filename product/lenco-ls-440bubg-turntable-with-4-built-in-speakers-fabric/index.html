Description The Lenco LS-440 is a turntable with built-in speakers. The 4 speakers, 2 x 15 watts and 2 x 10 watts, provide a well-balanced sound. The turntable is easy to use: simply plug it into the power outlet and you can immediately enjoy your favourite LP! Or stream your playlist from your smartphone or tablet via Bluetooth® 5.0 to the speakers in the turntable. If you want to retreat and relax with your favourite music, simply connect a pair of headphones. Perfect for LPs and Singles With adjustable speed, you can choose between 33 and 45 RPM and easily play singles and LPs. The LS-440 is a belt-driven turntable and is equipped with a high-quality Audio-Technica cartridge, ensuring optimal audio quality and prolonging the lifespan of your LPs. With Start-Stop Function The start-stop button allows you to stop the record at any time and resume later exactly where you left off. Built-In Preamplifier The Lenco LS-440 features a preamplifier that can be switched on and off. This turntable also includes pitch control, allowing you to manually adjust the playback speed, as well as tone control, enabling you to adjust the highs and lows. With Plastic Dust Cover The plastic dust cover ensures that your turntable does not get covered in dust. The Lenco LS-440 is finished with a beautiful fabric and is available in anthracite or beige/light blue. Integrated High-Quality Speakers: The Lenco LS-440 features a built-in speaker system consisting of two 15 Watt and two 10-Watt speakers, providing a total output power of 50 Watts. This configuration ensures a balanced and high-quality sound output, ideal for an immersive vinyl listening experience. Wireless Connectivity and User-Friendly Operation: Equipped with Bluetooth® 5.0, the turntable offers the convenience of wireless streaming from smartphones or tablets. Advanced Audio Components and Controls: The turntable includes a high-quality Audio-Technica moving magnet cartridge (MMC) and a belt drive mechanism for optimal sound quality and vinyl record preservation.
